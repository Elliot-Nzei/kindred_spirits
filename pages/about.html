Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password from the Account Settings section of your Soul Profile. Look for the "Change Password" option.

Where can I find guided meditations?

Our guided meditations are available in the Practice Studio. You can filter by duration, type, and intention.

How do I connect with other members?

Visit the Community Feed Dashboard to see posts from other members. You can send connection requests from their Soul Profile pages.

Contact Us

If you can't find what you're looking for, please reach out to our support team.
